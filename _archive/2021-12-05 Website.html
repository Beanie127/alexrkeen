Alex Keen

A writer, an award-winning improv comedian and and a designer of TTRPGs, Alex Keen lives in Sheffield, England with his partner and no pets or children, thank you.

  • I co-created the award-winning Sex, Lies & Improvisation
  • I co-created MATES: The Improvised ‘90s Sitcom
  • I founded and coach Stürike Comedy
  • I’m heavily involved in improv in Sheffield
  • I’m a long-running participant, teacher and welfare team rep at the British Improv Project
  • I edit and write for Steel City Improv, a Sheffield-centric improv blog.
  • I occasionally release RPG stuff here
  • and I make a living as Head of Communications and Marketing at Sent Into Space.

I’m not on most social media, and any I can’t avoid are purely for professional purposes at this point in my life. If you want to contact me, reach out here.
