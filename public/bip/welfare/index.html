Wellbeing, Health & Safety

by Alex Keen

Hi, we're Alex, Bobby, Carly, Jen & Jo and we are your Welfare team for this BIP weekend. We're here for you to talk to if you have any concerns or issues with anything at BIP, or if you just fancy a chat. Come and find us at any point over the weekend — we'll be the ones wearing pink BIP shirts.

Incident reporting

To report an urgent incident, please find one of us, Geoff or Tom. If you cannot find a person, call Alex on 0751 772 3666. For non-urgent incidents or complaints, please use the form below. There is no required information and we will respect any requests for privacy, but the more you are willing to share, the better we will be equipped to solve any immediate or ongoing problems and make things better in the future.

Code of conduct and behaviour at the venue

  • The BIP code of conduct is available on our website and has been emailed to you.
  • The venue sells drinks. Do not consume your own in public areas such as the lobby, be discreet.
  • Do not leave cans, bottles and glasses in the function rooms after a show, please clean up your own drink empties, snacks etc and take venue glasses back to the bar.
  • Please address any event concerns to the reception immediately if there is a problem with your room or to a food and beverage manager if you have a concern about the food. Also make Geoff aware and we'll try to resolve the issue. It will be too late after the event!
  • Let us all maintain an environment in which people are treated with dignity, decency and respect. Our welfare team is made up of volunteers who have all attended most of the BIP events since 2015.

Trigger warning: noise

At various points over the weekend, 90 improvisers will be in one room together — this can get very noisy. Please be aware of this if you have sound sensitivities, especially during warm up/get to know you games, or the evening entertainment.
