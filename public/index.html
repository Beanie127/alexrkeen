Alex Keen

Hi, I'm Alex, a storyteller, designer and strategist living in Sheffield, England with my husband and no children or pets, thank you.

I spend my working hours at Sent Into Space, an aerospace creative agency. I manage communications, marketing and brand strategy, but in typical SME fashion, my work also spans sales, product development, operations and change management.

My vocational practices include writing fiction, non-fiction and essays; performing, teaching and organising improv comedy and theatre; designing tabletop roleplaying games; graphic design and web design.

I love thinking and talking about imagination, stories, games and play; the psychology and philosophy of living well; and how we can replace extractive, growth-obsessed corporations with small, self-sufficient, sustainable communities.

I don't like social media. If you want to talk, email alexrkeen(delete this)@gmx.com.
